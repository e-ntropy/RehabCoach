    • How it works

    Your personal rehabilitation coach can be started by 3 simple steps.

    01

    Tell us about you by logging in or registering.

    We have built a user management system by using Mysol, which allows us to keep track of your behavior if you log in or register at the first time, making it easier for you to use the software next time.

    02

    Turn on the camera to start your first muscle bone rehabilitation training

    We use various models to determine whether your movements are standard and provide guidance and suggestions to help you efficiently achieve muscle bone rehabilitation training. But as a prerequisite for all of this, please open the camera permission on your phone for easy estimation of human posture

    03

    Changing the accuracy of the model and screen resolution to improve the user experience

    We provide different model accuracy and screen resolution to adapt to different performance phones, adjust these data to improve your user experience, as the old saying goes, what suits you is the best.
